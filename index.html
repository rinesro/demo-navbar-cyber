Super Easy Customization with CSS Variables

You don't need to touch hundreds of lines of code. Just change a few variables at the top of the file. style.css to create your own unique theme.

:root {
        --primary-color: cyan;
        --primary-glow-color: rgba(0, 255, 255, 0.3);
        --background-gradient-start: #0f2027;
        --background-gradient-middle: #203a43;
        --background-gradient-end: #2c5364;
        --text-color: #ffffff;
        --dark-color: #000000;
        --font-family: 'Orbitron', sans-serif;
    }
